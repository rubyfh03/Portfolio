Crafters Corner

I created a tutorial-based blog for the popular game Minecraft with a focus on
simplicity, ease of use, and elegance. Inspired by the principles outlined in
the book Don't Make Me Think, I designed the website to ensure an intuitive
and user-friendly experience.

The planning phase involved creating wireframes and mockups using Figma, a
tool I now incorporate into all my projects. Its versatility and efficiency
make it an invaluable asset in bringing my design ideas to life.
